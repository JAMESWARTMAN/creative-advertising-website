About Us

Creative Advertising was born to help businesses, and nonprofits, grow. We are a growing digital agency representing a fusion of modern and strategic marketing practices. We are headquartered in North Olmsted, Ohio with a client presence in Ohio and beyond. Our clients span across industries and also include international nonprofits and social enterprises.
